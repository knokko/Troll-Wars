The health starts at maxHealth or the current health.
Health will be reduced when the creature is attacked.
When the health is zero or lesser, the creature will faint.

The mana starts at maxMana or the current mana.
Some moves cost mana to cast.
When there is not enough mana left, not all moves can be used.

The focus starts at 0.
Casting some spells successfully increases focus.
Getting attacked reduces focus.
The more focus, the stronger magic moves will be.

The energy starts at maxEnergy.
Some moves cost energy to use.
Energy regenerates a little every turn.
When there is not enough energy left, not all moves can be used.

The rage starts at 0.
Some moves require rage, cost rage or are stronger with more rage.
Getting attacked increases rage.
Attacking reduces rage.
